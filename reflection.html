Reflection

This part illustrates the reflection of other group members and the group work. It contains people's thinking of what goes well during the work, which part should be improve in the work, what surpriting the individual the most during the work and what can people learn form the work.

Yi Yang reflection

What went well is that everyone in the group makes contributions for the assignment and members are friendly and efficiency during the meeting as well as during the communication. People are not hesitating to ask questions or figure our others’ mistake which is really helpful for keeping the progress of the assignment.

What could be improve is that people could communicate even more to always let others know the status of each other. For example, if we set a due date for a task and people can updated the status of the task before due date in the chat box rather than show the whole result at the end. In this way, if there are certain mistakes, others can figure out them in time.

What was surprising me is also what I can learn from the group. Members’ project ideas are full of imagination. They are not limited to the realization of software applications but are more about the combination of hardware and software. Even though this may require more skills and knowledge, I learn to think ideas and solve problems from multiple angles.
